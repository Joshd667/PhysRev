Install App

Install as Progressive Web App

Install this app on your device for a native app experience. You'll be able to access it offline, get faster loading times, and use it just like a regular app.

iOS/Safari Installation:
  1. 1. Tap the Share button at the bottom
  2. 2. Select "Add to Home Screen"
  3. 3. Tap "Add" to confirm
Benefits of Installing:
  • Works offline - access your data without internet
  • Faster loading - instant startup from home screen
  • Native feel - fullscreen experience without browser UI
  • App icon - quick access from your home screen
